BASIC STATISTICAL CONCEPT

* POPULATION:

This can be defined as the entire group of individuals or cases that you are interested in studying.

* SAMPLE:

This can be defined as the entire or representative part of the population, This is a subset of individuals or cases selected from the population.

* VARIABLE:

This is a characteristic or attitude that is being measured or observed.

* DATA:

This are the actual values of measurements obtained from the sample or population.

* PARAMETER:

This is the numerical value that describes the characteristics of a population.

* STATISTIC:

This is the numerical value that describes the characteristics of a sample.

* STATISTICS:

This is defined as the science of Collection, Presentation, Analysis and Interpretation of numerical data.

COLLECTION OF DATA

This is the process of gathering information from various sources for the purpose of making an enquiry, Study or Research.

TYPES OF DATA

1. Primary Data
2. Secondary Data

1. Primary Data: This are data collected by the investigator himself for the purpose of specific enquiry or Study.
Such data is original in character and has never been used by anybody.
Primary data can be collected by the following methods.

i. Direct Personal Interface
ii. Indirect Oral Interface
iii. Schedule Sent Through Enumerators
iv. Mailed Questionaire
